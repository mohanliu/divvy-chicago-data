Each marker represents a divvy bike station (based on its GPS coordinates). The size of each marker denotes the bicycle usage of that station.¶

The difference between the trip start and trip end is not significant, even though only 3.17% of biker return their bikes to the start stations.¶

However, the bike usages between weekdays and weekends are quite different. During weekdays, there are high demands of bikes in the Loop Area of Chicago, where big companies locate. During weekends, people tend to rent bike closer to the Lake Michigan.¶
